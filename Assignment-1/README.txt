Key Bindings:


Left Mouse Click - Start Painting
Right Mouse Click - Stop Painting

Left Arrow - Decrease opacity
Right Arrow - Increase Opacity

Up Arrow - Increase Radius
Down Arrow - Decrease Radius

Number 	- Colour
1	- White
2 	- Red
3 	- Blue
4 	- Yellow
5 	- Cyan
6 	- Magenta
7 	- Maroon
8 	- Green
9 	- Purple
0 	- Black
